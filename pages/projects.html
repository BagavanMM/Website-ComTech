About Me

Hey, I'm Bagavan Marakathalingasivam! I'm a Grade 11 student attending Unionville High School in Markham, Ontario. I'm incredibly passionate about the intersection of creative art and technology. Throughout this semester and the school year, my goals are to excel academically, actively participate in STEM-related clubs and competitions, and explore internship opportunities to gain practical experience in my field of interest.

On a personal level, I'm constantly inspired by the limitless possibilities in the world of technology. I find great joy in combining art and technology to express myself through various digital media and interactive experiences. It's fascinating to me how innovation and creativity can come together to create something truly impactful. I'm also motivated by influential figures in the tech industry who continuously push boundaries and make positive contributions to society.
