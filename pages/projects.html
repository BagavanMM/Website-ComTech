Explore my Creative Projects

Now we're at the good part! Here are the three main projects that I've built and collaborated on over the course of this semester. Each project focuses on a different form of media, from poster design, to photography to videography.

Poster

Mental Health IS a Big Deal

One of the first projects I created, this poster, designed in Adobe Illustrator, was meant to help educate teenagers and get rid of the stigma behind mental health. Ultiamtely, enablign more teens to speak up about thier issues and seek the help that they need.

Photography

Capturing Emotion through a Lens

This was a group project where my partner, Jacky Guo, and I shot and edited 12 different photos to represent the different camera angles and styles to ultiamtely portray different emotions and messages to the audience.

Videography

The Office on a Budget - Stapler in jello

One of our final projects, where we had to recreate a scene from one of our favourite TV Shows or Movies. We chose the Office, specifically the scene where Jim puts Dwight's stapler in Jello. We had to work with what we had and managed to create a pretty accurate "on a budget" version of this scene.
